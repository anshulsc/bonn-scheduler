# 🌐 STEP 2B: Mandatory Web Intelligence & Live Data Enrichment

## 🚨 IMPORTANT RULE

You MUST NOT rely only on local files.

For EVERY course, you MUST actively use:

* Web search
* Official university pages
* Professor websites
* Lab/research group pages

---

## 🔍 What You MUST Search For Each Course

For each course, perform web searches to find:

### 1. 📘 Official Course Page

* University of Bonn course page
* Lecture page (current or past offering)

Extract:

* Syllabus
* Weekly topics
* Slides / materials (if public)
* Exam format

---

### 2. 👨‍🏫 Professor Information

Identify the instructor and search for:

* Personal academic webpage
* Google Scholar profile (if needed)

Extract:

* Research areas
* Recent publications
* Teaching style (if inferable)

---

### 3. 🧪 Associated Lab / Research Group

Find:

* Lab or institute associated with the course

Extract:

* Research focus of the lab
* Ongoing projects
* Relevance to:

  * NLP
  * ML
  * Vision
  * Robotics

---

### 4. 📊 Course Context

Search for:

* Whether course is core / advanced / niche
* Student feedback (if available)
* How often it is offered

---

## 🧠 Enhanced Course Profile (MANDATORY UPDATE)

For each course, ADD:

### 🌐 Online Resources

* Official course link
* Lecture page (if available)

### 👨‍🏫 Instructor Insight

* Name
* Research focus
* Alignment with student goals

### 🧪 Lab Connection

* Lab name
* Research areas
* Whether joining this course helps entering the lab

---

## 🎯 Strategic Insight (VERY IMPORTANT)

After gathering data, analyze:

* Does this course help in getting a **HiWi position** in that lab?
* Is this professor relevant for:

  * Thesis
  * Research collaboration
* Is this a **gateway course** into a research group?

---

## ⚠️ Priority Rules

Give HIGH PRIORITY to courses that:

* Are taught by professors in:

  * ML
  * NLP
  * Vision
  * Robotics
* Belong to active research labs
* Provide project-based work

---

## 🚫 Avoid Weak Data

If:

* No official page exists
* Data is outdated

Then:

* Use multiple sources
* Cross-verify information

---

## 🧠 Final Goal of This Step

Transform each course from:

❌ “Just a subject”

➡️ Into:

✅ A **research opportunity**
✅ A **lab entry point**
✅ A **PhD stepping stone**
